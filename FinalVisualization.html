About this dataset and visualization.

Dataset: 535,396 timestamped records of slang term usage across six platforms (TikTok, Twitter, Reddit, Instagram, YouTube, Twitch), annotated with term category, user age group, origin platform, usage context, and a continuous sentiment score (−1 to +1).

Scope of this visualization: Filtered to the three intent categories most relevant to social engagement mechanics — approval (terms that affirm or celebrate), emphasis (terms that intensify or assert), and insult (terms that demean or mock) — totaling 202,164 records.

Sentiment classification: Positive = avg score ≥ 0.15 · Neutral = −0.15 to 0.15 · Negative = ≤ −0.15. The Top Terms tab shows all terms regardless of category filter.

Heatmap coloring: Each view is independently normalized. Cell color intensity maps the raw count linearly from the minimum to maximum value within that view. Row-share percentages in tooltips are calculated relative to that row's total.

Note on emphasis terms: Only two terms appear in the emphasis category in this dataset (no cap, periodt), which explains the notably smaller volume compared to approval and insult.
