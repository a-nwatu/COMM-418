<h1> Final Project Datset: Gen-Z Slang Tracker Data </h1>

<p>
For the purposes of my final project, I used a synthetic data set from Kaggle: GenZ Slang Evolution Track (2020-2025) created by Likitha Gedipudi. The last update made to the dataset was done three months ago, and it accesible via the following link:

https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/likithagedipudi/genz-slang-evolution-tracker-2020-2025

According to the description on the website, it has captured 535,000 synthetic records tracking the complete lifecycle of 46 Gen-Z slang terms spanning from 2020 to 2025, and across several social media platforms: TikTok, Twitter, Instagram, Reddit, Youtube, Twitch, and Discord. The purpose of the dataset is primarily for sentiment and demographic analysis.

Out of 22 available variables, my analysis only required ten: usage platform, term category, user age group, origin platform, usage context, and sentient score (-1 to +1 scale). The four main parts of the visualization are: three heat-maps (Age by Platform, Age by Intent, and Platform by Intent) and six top terms lists per platform.
